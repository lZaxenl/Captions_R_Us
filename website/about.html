Why is it important?

Close captions allow deaf and hard-of-hearing viewers to consume your videos, broadening your audience and demonstrating a commitment to digital inclusion.
Improving language skills as people will learn and also consume more videos as it will be better understood.
More than half of students surveyed said captions help by improving comprehension. The most common reasons students use captions are to helo them focus. retain information, and overcome the poor audio quality of the videos
